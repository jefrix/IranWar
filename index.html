About the publication

Notes from a moment of rupture

Fieldnotes is a publication for long-form writing about the world as it is actually shaped — not as headlines describe it, and not as models predict it, but as it behaves under stress. The dispatches here range across geopolitics, history, science, and current events, because the questions that matter rarely respect the boundaries of a single field.

The method is consistent even when the subject changes: take a problem seriously, trace its structure, look for the load-bearing variables, commit to a reading of what is happening, and hold that reading loosely enough to revise it when the evidence shifts. Every dispatch is a structured guess. None of them are predictions.

New dispatches appear when something is worth saying. There is no schedule, no newsletter, no paywall. Just the writing, and whatever thinking it provokes.
